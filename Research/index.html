Research

Welcome to the Biometrics and Visual Computing Research Group（BVCRG）

We are part of the Beijing Key Laboratory of Super Intelligent Technology for Urban Architecture at School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Beijing University of Civil Engineering and Architecture.

Research interests

  • Trustworthy Biometrics, specially on iris/ocular recognition, presentation attack detection (or liveness detection), synthetic image generation and detection, person recognition in long-range and wide-area scenarios

  • Air-Space-Ground Multi-modal Visual Computing

  • Urban Spatiotemporal Foundation Model and Generative AI

  • AI for Engineering
